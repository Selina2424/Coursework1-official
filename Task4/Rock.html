Rock Music

What is Rock music

Rock music is harsher and often self-consciously more serious.
It heavily focuses on guitar, drums, and powerful vocals.
It is sometimes also known as "rock and roll"

Started in: 1960s
Originated from:
United States

Random Rock facts

  • Folk rock, such as that made popular by Bob Dylan in the 1960s, often featured acoustic guitars and socially conscious lyrics, many with anti-war sentiments.
  • Rock has also led the way for other music forms, such as heavy metal, which features extreme guitar sound and heavy distortion.
  • It can refer to anything from soft pop to heavy metal
