Rap Music

What is Rap music

Rap music is sometimes known as hip-hop music.
Rap musicians generally perform in a vocal style in which the lyrics are spoken, rather than sung.
It is popular among people of all ages and backgrounds.

Started in: 1970s
Originated from:
United States

Random Rap facts

  • Busta Rhymes was the voice of Reptar on Rugrats.
  • Lauryn Hill got booed while performing at The Apollo.
  • The cover of Enter The Wu-Tang (36 Chambers) didn't feature all the members of Wu-Tang.
