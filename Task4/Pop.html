Pop Music

What is Pop music

It is usually played on electronic instruments
It consists of short songs with a strong beat and simple tunes
It is a genre of popular music that produces the most hits

Started in: 1950s
Originated from:
United States, United Kingdom

Random Pop facts

  • Elvis Presley was a rock and roll musician and was one of the first worldwide pop music stars that we know today.
  • The Beatles were one of the first "boy bands" in pop music, bands such as Take That, One Direction, JLS and many more have them to thank!
  • In the UK, 178 songs have sold over 1 million copies.
